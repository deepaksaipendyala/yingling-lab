flory_fox_scraper
Python · Machine Learning

Scrapes glass transition temperature (Tg) data for polymers and performs Flory-Fox fitting. Enables data-driven prediction of polymer thermal properties from literature.

lammps-mspin
C++ · LAMMPS Plugin

LAMMPS plugin for atomistic-resolution simulation of magnetic nanoparticles. Implements Zeeman alignment and dipolar assembly physics for spin-active systems in molecular dynamics.

Akhlak U. Mahmood & Yaroslava G. Yingling
ACS J. Chemical Theory & Computation (2022) · DOI: 10.1021/acs.jctc.1c01253

CALB Variants — Lipase with Improved Synthesis Activity
K. Clendennen, Y. G. Yingling, H. Kim

Amino acid sequence variants of a lipase with improved activity for catalyzing synthesis reactions. Methods include predicting amino acid sites for change based on computational models of protein structure in non-aqueous conditions. Described enzyme variants show a 3- to 9-fold improvement in synthesis activity over the parent protein sequence.

MatterX
Redefining What Matter Can Do
Founded · AI Polymers · Self-Driving Labs

MatterX creates autonomous platforms that design and synthesize next-generation polymers through the convergence of advanced robotics, artificial intelligence, and computational chemistry. Its self-driving laboratories compress traditional polymer R&D from years to weeks — running 10,000+ experiments per month with 95% prediction accuracy on polymer properties.

MatterX's closed-loop system spans AI-driven molecular design, autonomous robotic synthesis, and real-time characterization feedback — with applications in sustainable materials, high-performance composites, biomedical devices, and energy storage.

SustainAI
Local-first AI for agriculture
Founded 2025 · Piloting · sustain-ai.net

SustainAI delivers private, on-site AI infrastructure for farms and water utilities — combining field sensor data, scientific knowledge, and domain context into a modular system that generates decision-ready alerts directly on site, offline-capable, without cloud dependency.

The system connects to existing field hardware (water monitors, soil sensors, weather stations), runs fine-tuned language models and knowledge graph pipelines entirely on-premises, and outputs simple, explainable recommendations — flagging water quality anomalies, fertilization risks, livestock disease indicators, and regulatory compliance issues before they escalate. Powered by the PASTOR architecture (Phosphorus AI for Sustainability, Traceability, and Off-grid Reasoning), developed with the NSF STC STEPS Center. Currently piloting with 3–5 farms and water utilities.
